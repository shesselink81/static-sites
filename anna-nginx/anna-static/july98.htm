ANNAMANIACS: NEWS ARCHIVE : JULY 1998
July was a quiet month for Anna & her fans. Because of an Injury at her thumb , she wasn't able to play any tournament.

Kournikova still not able to play. 17-year old Russian has withdrawn from the A&P Classics in Mahwah , because of too much pain in her right thumb. The A&P Classics should have been Kournikova's comeback. The Russian attained a serious injury at the Eastborne Tournament in June and hasn't played since. 16th July.


Anna's WTA position July'98 : 12
