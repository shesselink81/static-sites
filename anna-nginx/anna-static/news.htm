Anna wins again in plays Graf in round 4.
PARIS (May 29, 1999) - In the 3rd round of the French    Open, Anna prevailed, 6-1, 3-6, 6-0 victory over the    11th-seed Patty Schnyder. Later, Schnyder said she had played badly because of her well-known dislike for   Kournikova.  
 After the match, Kournikova wouldn't speak about bad feelings between her and
 Schnyder."I don't know her personally at all. There are a thousand players on 
 the tour," she said.

 Anna's win earned her a round of 16 showdown with No. 6 seed Steffi Graf, who 
 beat Asa Carlsson of Sweden 6-1, 6-4. They will play on Sunday (2nd match on 
 Centre Court).
