The battle of teenage girls. There's no better description about today's women's tennis scene. The level of women's tennis is getting higher and higher. The players are younger on top and the game is getting faster and faster. Reason for all these changes are teenage pro players. Currently Venus Williams (17) , Martina Hingis (17), Anna Kournikova (16), Mirjana Lucic (16) and Serena Williams (15) are fighting for that desirable No. 1 position on the COREL WTA list. Hingis has already achieved this goal , but the other girls don't think she going to be number 1 for a long time. That's because they all think that the number one position belongs............to theirselves. Click on the name of Anna's rivals to read about their careers. And about the question who's going to be number ONE : no doubt about that : Anna Kournikova.
