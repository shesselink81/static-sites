ANNAMANIACS: NEWS ARCHIVE : JUNE 1998
June is the month of Anna's birthday. She's 17 now and that means she's allowed to play all the tournaments she wants. But after the birthdayparty , Anna had to deal with a very great dissapointment : because of a serious thumb-injury , she has to skip Wimbledon. The greatest tournament in Tennis.


No Wimbledon for Anna this year  !!!!! Anna has to skip Wimbledon because of an injury at her right-thumb. Anna's said to be very disappointed. Better luck next year.

Also good news today: Anna made her debut in the WTA Top 10. She's now world's number 10.
22 june 1998.


Anna retires at the Eastborne Tournament. During her semi-final match against Sanchez- Vicario she felt to much pain in her hand. Anna said she'll do everything to appear at Wimbledon this monday , but the injurie is serious.  June 20th 1998
